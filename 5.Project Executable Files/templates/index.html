Bone Fracture Detection

To detect bone fractures, X-ray images are captured, preprocessed, and analyzed using the YOLOv8 model. The model detects and localizes the fracture, generates a bounding box, classifies the fracture, and assigns a probability score. The results are then visualized and validated clinically to confirm accuracy.
